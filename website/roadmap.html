The Vision

LAUNCHR was built by degens, for degens. We believe meme coins should be fun, transparent, and accessible to everyone. No gatekeeping, no complicated setups.

We're building in public, iterating based on community feedback, and keeping it real about what this is: an experimental platform for meme coin culture on Solana.

"If the President can launch a meme coin, so can you."
